ABOUT NITESH KHARE

NITESH KHARE IS A PROMINENT ANIMAL WELFARE ACTIVIST IN INDIA, RENOWNED FOR HIS DEDICATION TO ANIMAL PROTECTION AND ADVOCACY. AS THE FOUNDER AND NATIONAL PRESIDENT OF THE SOCIETY FOR ANIMAL SAFETY (SAS) INDIA, HE LEADS A CORE ORGANIZATION COMMITTED TO ANIMAL WELFARE LITIGATION AND THE PROTECTION OF ANIMAL RIGHTS.

NITESH'S PASSION EXTENDS BEYOND ADVOCACY TO PRACTICAL SOLUTIONS. HE IS THE VISIONARY BEHIND FURGO, A PIONEERING PET TRANSPORTATION AND ANIMAL AMBULANCE SERVICE BASED IN PUNE, ENSURING SAFE AND COMPASSIONATE CARE FOR ANIMALS IN NEED.

ADDITIONALLY, NITESH IS THE FOUNDER OF BEFURS, INDIA’S FIRST PREMIUM CLOTHING BRAND DEDICATED TO ANIMAL AWARENESS. BEFURS COMBINES FASHION WITH A MISSION, OFFERING APPAREL ADORNED WITH UNIQUE ANIMAL-THEMED ILLUSTRATIONS AND QUOTES TO RAISE AWARENESS AND PROMOTE ANIMAL RIGHTS.

THROUGH HIS DIVERSE INITIATIVES, NITESH KHARE CONTINUES TO MAKE A SIGNIFICANT IMPACT ON ANIMAL WELFARE, BLENDING ACTIVISM WITH INNOVATION TO CREATE A BETTER WORLD FOR ANIMALS.

MISSION

NITESH KHARE’S MISSION IS TO ADVANCE ANIMAL WELFARE THROUGH COMPREHENSIVE ADVOCACY, INNOVATIVE SOLUTIONS, AND PUBLIC AWARENESS. BY LEADING THE SOCIETY FOR ANIMAL SAFETY (SAS) INDIA, HE STRIVES TO UPHOLD AND ENFORCE ANIMAL RIGHTS THROUGH LEGAL ACTION AND POLICY REFORM. THROUGH FURGO, HIS AIM IS TO PROVIDE VITAL, COMPASSIONATE CARE AND TRANSPORTATION FOR ANIMALS IN EMERGENCIES. WITH BEFURS, HE SEEKS TO BLEND FASHION WITH ACTIVISM, USING CREATIVE APPAREL TO INSPIRE AND EDUCATE THE PUBLIC ON ANIMAL RIGHTS ISSUES. HIS MISSION IS DRIVEN BY A COMMITMENT TO IMPROVING THE LIVES OF ANIMALS AND ENSURING THEIR PROTECTION AND DIGNITY.

VISSION

NITESH KHARE ENVISIONS A WORLD WHERE ANIMAL WELFARE IS A FUNDAMENTAL PRINCIPLE AND EVERY LIVING BEING IS TREATED WITH RESPECT AND COMPASSION. HE AIMS TO CREATE A SOCIETY WHERE ANIMAL RIGHTS ARE FULLY RECOGNIZED AND ENFORCED, AND WHERE INNOVATIVE SOLUTIONS AND COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT LEAD TO A HARMONIOUS COEXISTENCE BETWEEN HUMANS AND ANIMALS. HIS VISION INCLUDES A FUTURE WHERE EDUCATION AND AWARENESS THROUGH INITIATIVES LIKE BEFURS FOSTER A DEEPER CONNECTION BETWEEN PEOPLE AND ANIMALS, ULTIMATELY LEADING TO A MORE EMPATHETIC AND JUST WORLD FOR ALL CREATURES.
